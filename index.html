About me

I am a PhD student with a fellowship from Spanish Science Minitry at the GRAM group from University of Alcalá de Henares. I am advised by Roberto J. López Sastre. My work is focused on visual semantic navigation on novel environments. The main goal is to implement visual navigation algorithms into real robots. These robots are going to help children with neurological dissabilities using its navigation abilities. The robots developed in this project are finally donated to families thanks to Padrino Tecnológico Foundation.

Appart from my work, I really enjoy playing music. I played bass and drums in Madrid based hardcore band Brothers Till We Die (as you can see on the background image 🤠). I also like to do silly music with my friends and doing sports (gym, padel...).
